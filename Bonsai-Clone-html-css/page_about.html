Bonsai is building the global operating system for freelance work.

The way the world works is changing.

Companies want on-demand talent rather than full time employees. Workers want flexible and independent careers. This is one of the largest socio-economic shifts since the Industrial Revolution.

While the nature of work is changing rapidly, the tools and processes supporting it are stuck in the twentieth (or even nineteenth!) century.

Bonsai is dedicated to helping companies and independent talent work better together. We're building solutions to support a future where work is flexible and globally distributed.
